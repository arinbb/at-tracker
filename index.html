⛰ Welcome to AT Section Tracker

Track your progress along the 2,194-mile Appalachian Trail, plan upcoming hikes, and manage your pack — all in your browser, no account required.

🥾
Mark hiked sections
Tap a checkbox to log it. Use + Trip to mark a whole stretch in two clicks.
🗓
Plan a trip
Tap the bookmark icon on segments to plan them. Planned shows mileage, elevation, difficulty, and a day-by-day itinerary.
🎒
Plan your pack
Open ⋯ More → Pack to track gear weight and condition. Share or import a pack from a URL.
🔎
Search anything
Press / or click the magnifier to jump to any peak, town, shelter, or road crossing.

Sign in with Google (top right) to sync across devices. Your data is stored locally otherwise.
